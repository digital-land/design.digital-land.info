Context

The DLUHC service, RIPA, needs machine readable data about planning and housing to help their users submit valid planning applications.

Hypothesis

We believe

Local Planning Authorities (LPA) will need help with the task of providing the data needed by planning.data.gov.uk and RIPA.

We think this is because

Some LPAs have low access to the digital/data skills and resources needed to complete the tasks and provide the data we will ask of them.

So if we

  • Provide guidance about the data we require and how to provide it.
  • Provide a way for LPAs to inspect the data they have provided to planning.data.gov.uk
  • Provide a way for LPAs to get support.

We should see

  • Fewer errors and omissions in the data that is provided.
  • Less 1-1 support and assistance is required by LPAs.
  • The data can be used by digital-land and RIPA with little or no manual intervention.
